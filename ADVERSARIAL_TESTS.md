## Public Claim Rule

If evidence is not attached, use softer wording:

- “implemented”
- “in testing”
- “available in MVP”
- “planned”
- “under evaluation”

Do not use:

- “proven”
- “certified”
- “enterprise-grade”
- “production hardened”
unless evidence supports it.
