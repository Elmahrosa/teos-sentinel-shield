// Capabilities
Built for compliance from day one.

Safe Ingestion Engine is designed for AI pipelines, RAG systems, and data-sensitive applications that require auditable, policy-aware web ingestion.

🛡
SSRF Protection
Private IPs, loopback ranges, and metadata endpoints are blocked before outbound requests are made.
🧼
PII Scrubbing
Sensitive identifiers are redacted or hashed before content is returned or stored.
📜
robots.txt Enforcement
Policy checks are applied before fetch, not after, helping prevent non-compliant ingestion.
⚙️
Policy Engine
Domain rules, crawl limits, and safety controls are handled declaratively.
⚡
Async Pipeline
FastAPI, Redis, and Celery power asynchronous ingestion and job processing.
🧾
Audit Logging
Every request can be traced through a structured ingestion and policy trail.
